Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current vice-captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats. He was born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off-break bowler.

Rohit made his debut for the Indian cricket team in June 2007 and has since become one of the most successful openers in limited-overs cricket. He has several records to his name, including the highest individual score in One Day International (ODI) cricket (264 runs) and the most double centuries in ODI cricket (3).

Rohit has played for several domestic teams in India, including Mumbai and Deccan Chargers in the Indian Premier League. He has won numerous awards and accolades for his performances, including the prestigious Arjuna Award and the Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World award in 2019.
